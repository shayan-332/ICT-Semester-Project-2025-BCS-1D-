Our Story

The Passion Behind Chase Cheese

Chase Cheese was born from a simple love for authentic, cheesy pizza. Our founder, Shayan, grew up in a small Loonwaywala town where pizza was more than just food - it was a way of life.

After moving to FAST, he noticed a gap in the market for truly authentic, cheese-focused pizzas. Thus, Chase Cheese was established with the mission to bring the joy of perfectly melted, stretchy cheese to pizza lovers everywhere.

Today, we continue to honor those traditions while innovating with new flavors and techniques. Every pizza that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our commitment to quality and passion for great food.

Our team of dedicated chefs works tirelessly to perfect each recipe, ensuring that every bite delivers the perfect balance of flavors and that signature cheese pull that our customers love.
